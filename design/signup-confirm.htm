Other ways of signing up

If you would rather use email than this web interface, you can send an email to pika-lates@mit.edu with your diet and restrictions in the subject. The recognized words are:

vegan, v*, vegetarian, v, meat, m, glutenfree, gf, nonuts, nopeanuts

In the future, you will be able to save these preferences so you don't have to enter them every time.
